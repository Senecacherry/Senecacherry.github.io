Meet Alex

I have been passionate about music for many years; as soon as I learned to talk, I learned to sing. I have a great ear for sound, and I have a diverse background with many types of music.

It is my goal as an artist and as a musician to do my absolute best to convey a certain mood or feeling with my work, whether from my own creations or from music I record for others. Music has, and always will be a second language to me. I live and breathe to make sound the most versitile and meaningful medium of all, and I am confident that my skills and attitude will take myself and the people who work with me a long way.
